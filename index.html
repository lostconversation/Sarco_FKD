BIO

Italy based musician, producer and sound designer.
Co-founder of Yalla Records label, he’s been working as a producer on many records and composed soundtracks for videos and installations.


2012: his first USA tour

2013: Sarc:o has been awarded with the Golden A’Design Award for best music and sound design with the production of Chasing Space, soundtrack of the video by Marco Bagni, and contained in the album “Soundtracks Vol.1”.


2015: Sarc:o released the EP “Manifesto”, a break-through in Sarc:o’s music.


2017: a new album “Finding Knowing Delight”, featuring the lead singer Nicola Pressi, supported by a live tour with a full band.
